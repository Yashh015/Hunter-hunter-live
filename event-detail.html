About this event

A rooftop night you'll actually remember.

Sundowner Sessions is Vadodara's most-loved intimate music evening — back for its third volume with a handpicked lineup of local jazz musicians, a curated cocktail menu, and that golden-hour feeling we've all been chasing.

No big stage, no loud DJs — just great music, good people, and the city glowing below you. Expect impromptu jam sessions, signature sunset cocktails from The Terrace bar, and a curated cheese & charcuterie spread.

🎟️ Tickets include welcome drink · 🥂 Complimentary canapes for early birds · 📸 Photography encouraged
